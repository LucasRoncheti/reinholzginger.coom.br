Exportation

For almost a decade on the international scene, Reinholz Ginger is synonymous with tradition and excellence in the export of Brazilian ginger. Our relentless pursuit of quality has taken us to dozens of countries, and our special dedication is to provide unique and healthy flavors to Europe, where each piece of ginger tells a story of freshness and authenticity.

Tradition Breaking Borders
Our international journey is marked by tradition that transcends borders. Each country we reach is an extension of our passion for ginger, and our presence is recognized for the consistent quality we offer. From plantation to export, Reinholz Ginger is an ambassador of Brazilian flavor, carrying with it the rich heritage of a decade of experience.

Unique Flavors in Europe and Beyond
By focusing our efforts on Europe, we seek not only to export ginger, but to deliver a unique culinary experience. With each shipment, we aim to delight European palates with the freshness and authentic flavor of Brazilian ginger, promoting healthy and delicious choices. Join us on this journey, where Reinholz Ginger redefines excellence in ginger export to the world.
