Get to Know Our History

Committed to Quality and Innovation:


Raising the standard in ginger production and export, Reinholz Ginger stands out as a dynamic force, bringing freshness and authentic flavor to global markets. Established as a reference in this sector, our headquarters is strategically located to reflect the heart and soul of ginger cultivation in Brazil.


Pioneering Collaboration and Innovative Processes


Unlike a cooperative, Reinholz Ginger maintains a pioneering collaborative approach, working closely with committed farmers. Our specialized team meticulously monitors every phase of cultivation, processing, and distribution, ensuring the exceptional quality of every piece of ginger that reaches our customers around the world.


Excellence in Ginger Export and Beyond


Our mission goes beyond ginger export; it's a commitment to excellence in every aspect of our business. We constantly seek new alternatives to add value to our products, promoting innovations that reflect our commitment to quality, sustainability, and the appreciation of the culinary experience.


Mission, Vision, and Values of Reinholz Ginger


Mission: Enrich global culinary experiences, providing high-quality ginger with an innovative approach.


Vision: To be a recognized leader in ginger production and export, raising standards of quality and sustainability.


Values: Our actions are guided by ethics, innovation, commitment, socio-environmental responsibility, and the appreciation of the authenticity of each product.
